From Haarlemmerstraat walk to Central Station to go and have a look at the Skylounge on the eleventh floor of the Hilton Hotel. There is also seating outside so this is a great place to enjoy when the sun is shining with a view of the Amsterdam skyline on the one hand and of IJ river and the boats on the other. However in winter and at night you also have a great view of all the city lights. Be suprised what the skyline of Amsterdam has to offer you.
